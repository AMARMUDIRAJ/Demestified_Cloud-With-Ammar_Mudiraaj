# Content Calendar & Tracker

A simple plan to ship 110 videos without burning out. Pick a cadence, batch by domain, and check boxes as you go.

## Cadence options

| Cadence | Output | Full series finishes in |
|---|---|---|
| Casual | 1 video / week | ~2 years |
| **Recommended** | **2 videos / week** | **~1 year** |
| Aggressive | 3–4 videos / week | ~7–9 months |

> Tip: **record in batches by domain** (Foundation + 5 providers + comparison = ~7 videos) so wording, set, and energy stay consistent. Write all scripts for a domain first, then record back‑to‑back, then edit.

## Recommended launch order

1. **Phase 0** (8) — unlocks vocabulary for everything.
2. **Phase 1** (24) — cloud foundations + the 16 domain foundations.
3. **Phase 2** domain by domain in spine order: NET → CPT → STO → DB → IAM → SEC → SVR → OPS.
4. **Phase 3** (6) — projects, "which cloud?", certs, careers.

Publishing the foundations first means each Phase 2 provider video already has its prerequisite live.
