# SEO & Growth Playbook

How every video is titled, described, tagged, and packaged for discovery and retention.

## Titles

- Pair **curiosity + a searchable keyword**: `What Is a VPC? AWS Networking Explained for Total Beginners`.
- Lead with the searched term where possible ("AWS VPC", "Azure VM", "What is the cloud").
- Add a beginner signal: *for Beginners / Explained Simply / No Jargon / for Non‑IT*.
- Keep under ~60 characters of "punch" before any extra; front‑load the keyword.

**Per video, draft 3 title options** and pick the clearest.

## Descriptions

- **First 2 lines are the hook** (they show in search/suggested). Restate the promise + the analogy.
- Then: a short paragraph, **chapter timestamps**, links to the previous/next video in the spine, and the playlist link.
- Add a consistent footer: channel pitch, subscribe nudge, social links, and a hashtag set (`#CloudComputing #AWS #Azure #GCP #OracleCloud #AlibabaCloud #CloudForBeginners`).

## Tags / keywords (10–15 per video)

Mix three buckets:

1. **Concept:** `cloud computing`, `what is a VPC`, `virtual machine explained`, `object storage`.
2. **Provider:** `AWS for beginners`, `Azure tutorial`, `google cloud basics`, `OCI`, `alibaba cloud`.
3. **Audience/long‑tail:** `cloud computing for non IT`, `cloud for beginners no experience`, `learn cloud from scratch`.

## Thumbnails

- Use the **brand style** (dark gradient, cyan/violet glow, the cloud‑core mark).
- 3–5 word text, huge and legible on mobile. Example: "WHAT IS A VPC?".
- Show the **episode ID** (e.g., `P1-NET-F`) as a small chip for series consistency.
- Keep a consistent layout so the channel looks like a course, not random uploads.

## Chapters (every video)

Add timestamps in the description (`0:00 Hook`, `0:30 Recap`, …). Improves retention and gives Google rich results.

## Playlists (structure mirrors the spine)

- One playlist per **phase**: *Phase 0 — Digital Literacy*, *Phase 1 — Cloud Foundations*, etc.
- One playlist per **domain** in Phase 2 (Networking, Compute, Storage, …) so a learner can binge a topic across all five clouds.
- A flagship playlist: **"Cloud Computing from Zero — Full Path"** (all 110 in order).

## Pinned comment (every video)

End each video by asking **one** question and pin it to spark comments. Example for `P1-NET-F`:
> "Quick check: in our apartment‑building analogy, what's the *security guard*? First correct answer gets a shout‑out!"

## Cadence & momentum

- **Batch by domain** — script/record a whole domain (foundation + 5 providers + comparison) together for consistency.
- Publish on a **fixed weekly day/time**; announce the next title in the outro and description.
- See [`CONTENT-CALENDAR.md`](CONTENT-CALENDAR.md) for the schedule and tracker.

## End screens & cards

- End screen: point to the **next video in the spine** + subscribe.
- Card mid‑video: link the **Foundation** video when you reference it.
