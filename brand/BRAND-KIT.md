## Typography

- **Titles / thumbnails:** a bold geometric sans — **Montserrat**, **Poppins**, or **Sora** (weights 700–800).
- **Body / lower‑thirds:** **Inter** or **Roboto** (400–600).
- The provided SVGs use an Arial fallback so they render anywhere; swap in a brand font before final export for a more premium look.

## Do / Don't

- ✅ Keep backgrounds dark with neon cyan/violet accents. Plenty of contrast for text.
- ✅ Reuse the cloud‑core mark and the 5‑node motif across thumbnails and intros.
- ❌ Don't use official AWS/Azure/GCP/OCI/Alibaba logos or their trademark colors in a way that implies endorsement. Use neutral chips/labels (as in the banner).
- ❌ Don't crowd the banner's center safe area.
